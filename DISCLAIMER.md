# Trading Risk Disclaimer

MrRexoRR is software for visual trade planning and optional order submission in NinjaTrader 8. It does not provide financial, investment, trading, tax, or legal advice.

Futures trading involves substantial risk of loss and is not suitable for every trader. You are solely responsible for all orders, losses, platform configuration, broker configuration, data feed behavior, account selection, and execution results.

Before using any order submission feature:

- test on Playback or a non-live environment,
- verify the selected Chart Trader account,
- verify the instrument, expiry, quantity, OP, SL, and TP levels,
- verify OCO behavior in your own NinjaTrader installation,
- understand how your broker, prop firm, and data provider handle orders.

Known risk areas include, but are not limited to:

- rejected orders,
- missing or delayed market data,
- partial fills,
- duplicate clicks,
- platform reconnects,
- broker-side order handling differences,
- incorrect account selection,
- stale workspace state,
- unsupported NinjaTrader versions,
- user modifications to source code.
